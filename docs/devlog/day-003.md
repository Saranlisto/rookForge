# Day 003: Board Usability And Debugging

## Summary

Improved the board layer so positions are easier to inspect, debug, and round-trip through FEN.

## Square Indexing Convention

Squares use rank-major indexing from White's perspective:

- `a1` is index `0`
- `b1` is index `1`
- `h1` is index `7`
- `a2` is index `8`
- `h8` is index `63`

`Square` can now convert between file/rank coordinates, indexes, and algebraic notation such as `e4`.

## Board Display Format

Positions can be rendered as a simple text board:

```text
8 r n b q k b n r
7 p p p p p p p p
6 . . . . . . . .
5 . . . . . . . .
4 . . . . . . . .
3 . . . . . . . .
2 P P P P P P P P
1 R N B Q K B N R
  a b c d e f g h
```

Uppercase pieces are White, lowercase pieces are Black, and empty squares are `.`.

## FEN Round-Trip Support

`Position::to_fen()` serializes all six standard FEN fields:

1. piece placement
2. side to move
3. castling rights
4. en passant target
5. halfmove clock
6. fullmove number

Tests now cover round trips for the starting position, empty board, black-to-move positions, no castling rights, en passant targets, and a mixed position.

## Local Board Command

Added a debugging command for quickly inspecting FEN positions:

```bash
cargo run -- board --fen startpos
cargo run -- board --fen "8/8/8/8/8/8/8/8 w - - 0 1"
```

The `startpos` shortcut resolves to the standard starting FEN.

## Local Checks

`make check` now also runs the board debug command smoke checks.
